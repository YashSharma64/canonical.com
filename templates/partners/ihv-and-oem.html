Benefits of the IHV and OEM Partner Programme

Certification

  • Promote your hardware as 'Ubuntu Certified'
  • Assistance to test, review, and configure machines to be Ubuntu Certified
  • Public postings on our Ubuntu Certified hardware page

Sales enablement access

  • Opportunity registration
  • Sales assets
  • Pricing and packaging lists
  • Sales training

Joint marketing

  • Tailored campaigns
  • Access technical guides to enhance team's knowledge
  • Marketing funding for joint events
  • Logos and joint branding

Dedicated partner team

  • Named resources within Canonical for alliances, sales, technical training, marketing and certification

Reference architecture

  • Develop joint hardware-based reference architectures for predictable and repeatable customer deployments

Roadmap alignment

  • Weekly alignment calls with alliances and certification team
  • Bi-annual product roadmap review sessions to plan for upcoming releases

Sales

Increase your sales by becoming Ubuntu Certified

Our users have a high level of expectation in their experience with Ubuntu. Whether at home, in development, or production, an easy and predictable user experience is important to us. To ensure this success, we partner closely with server vendors worldwide to test, validate, and certify their portfolios.

The top 7 server manufacturers in the world are all regularly engaged with Canonical on promoting their platforms as Ubuntu Certified.
